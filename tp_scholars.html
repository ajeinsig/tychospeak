SKOLL
Clan librarian and vocal BeastClan advocate, Skoll takes great care of the Peak's archived documents and organizes papers written by the resident dragons. She can pick up a good scroll on nearly any subject.
TLALLI
Tlalli despises her Lightning Flight roots, and has instead travelled to the Starwood Strand and nearby Tycho's Peak to pursue her love of botany. She has since published many papers on the subject.
ADAMINE
Adamine was once an accomplished medic, but tragedy struck when her fragile mind failed her. She now assists novice medic Mortem as she slowly returns to her former mental state.
MORTEM
A former coroner who published studies on the effects of Arcane magic on the body, Mortem hesitantly accepted the position of medic at Tycho's Peak with the help of his companion, Adamine.
ZAZRA
A brilliant and detailed mapmaker, Zazra spends her time at Tycho's Peak mapping the constellations and planets found in the beautiful Arcane night sky.
TERPSICHORE
Despite her love of dance and her former life as a high-class courtesan, Terpsichore's main role at Tycho's Peak is utulizing her brilliant math skills to help her fellow scholars solve complicated equations.
HYAKU
Bold and brash, this bright young scholar tests the wits (and patience) of Tycho's Peak members with his confident yet brilliant insights on the nature of comets.
