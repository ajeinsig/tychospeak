ABOUT

Tycho’s Peak (pronounced TIE-co’s peak) is an Arcane clan nestled in the Crystalspine Reaches, bordering both the Starwood Strand and the Western Shore. Valencia, its Mirror matriarch, has a faulty memory, a love of discovery, and a fiercely loyal disposition when it comes to Arcane ideals. She instills these in the dragons of her clan, and is dedicated to turning Tycho’s Peak into a hub of scholars and a place of learning that the Arcanist himself would be proud of. Her loving mate, Beo the Guardian, keeps the peace and counters Valencia’s intense and often eccentric nature.

This scholarly clan exports various scientific papers, scrolls, artifacts, and books of Arcane origin (some written by Tycho’s Peak dragons themselves), especially since the clan regularly exchanges these materials with the Observatory’s library and outside clans. The Peak also exports scientific instruments crafted by its own artisans. Microscopes, telescopes, scales, globes, Bunsen burners, hourglasses, compasses, and glassware are all crafted within and traded with scientists all around Sornieth. As such, Tycho’s Peak is always looking for new sources of raw materials (gold, copper, silver, iron, silica), food, clothing, and, of course, new clans with which to exchange scrolls and artifacts for the sake of scientific discovery.
